About CareCrew

CareCrew is a team of passionate engineers and data scientists participating in the EY Techathon. Our mission is to revolutionize the automotive industry through intelligent, data-driven solutions.

Our Vision

We believe that vehicle maintenance should be proactive, not reactive. By harnessing the power of Agentic AI, we aim to eliminate unexpected breakdowns and optimize fleet operations for efficiency and sustainability.

The Team

We are a diverse group with expertise in machine learning, full-stack development, and automotive engineering. We are united by our drive to solve complex problems and deliver tangible value.
